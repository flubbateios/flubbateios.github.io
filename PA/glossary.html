Glossary

Here are some common words that are used to refer to things in PA.
Mex -> Metal Extractor
T2/Tier 2 -> Advanced
PGen -> Energy Plant
Com/Comm -> Commander
Cheese -> Unconventional strategy that usually relies on lack of scouting that is meant to catch the opponent by surprise
CSG -> Things on a map like mountains and platforms
Spaghetti -> Units that are going in one by one
